Research

Navigation bar

The navigation bar in BonsaiVerse has the links on the left-hand side, the logo in the center, and the search bar and user icon on the right-hand side similar to the reference webpage. However, the hover effect in our navigation bar changes color when the cursor hovers over it. On the click of the logo in the navigation bar, the user is redirected to the home page of BonsaiVerse. The font and the search box are kept with our design in mind.

Product Page

The product page of BonsaiVerse has a side navigation bar and rows of products displayed on the right similar to the reference webpage. Contrasting with the reference webpage, the webpage has a header and the products zoom on hover and an add to cart button pops up on hover. The side navigation bar shows the categories of the bonsai plants and each category is linked with a row in the products section.

Customer Review

The structure and layout of the customer review is very identical to the referenced webpage but the feel of the BonsaiVerse customer review is quite discrete. The review boxes have a rounded edge compared to the referenced webpage. The customer review section is on top of a background image.

Footer Section

The layout of the footer of BonsaiVerse and the referenced webpage is very much alike but the context in the footer is different. The right side has a newsletter sign-in box. It is then followed by quick links to various web pages which is similar to the referenced webpage, then comes the address of BonsaiVerse. Lastly, the right side has a link to all the socials of BonsaiVerse.

Timer Section

The timer shows when an ongoing sale is going to end and is located in the sale bar section right above the products. The timer of the BonsaiVerse shows the time in 00D:00H:00M:00S format and the referenced webpage shows the time in 00:00:00 format. The context with the timer of BonsaiVerse has a larger text size to grab the user's attention.
